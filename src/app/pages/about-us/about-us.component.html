Our Mission

To guide aspiring and experienced professionals into meaningful tech careers by removing barriers through mentorship, experience, and support.

Our Vision

To build a self-sustaining, diverse ecosystem of tech talent across Africa and beyond — powered by community, innovation, and opportunity.

Who We Support

  • 📚 Students in Higher Institutions
  • 💼 Career Switchers
  • 👩🏽‍💻 Early-Career Professionals
  • 🌍 Underrepresented Tech Talent

Why We Exist

PathToTech exists to bridge the opportunity gap. The tech industry can be overwhelming, gatekept, or isolating for newcomers — especially in underserved regions. We believe in demystifying the path, making tech education accessible, and unlocking opportunities through real people and real support.
