Your Bridge Into the Tech Industry

Our Mission

We’re here to empower aspiring and experienced professionals with the tools and support needed to succeed in the tech world. From students to career-changers, we help our members transition, grow, and lead in the industry.

Our Vision

To build a self-sustaining, diverse ecosystem of tech talent—driven by collaboration, mentorship, and innovation.

Who We Support

  • Professionals Transitioning into Tech
  • Experienced Tech Practitioners Seeking New Roles

Why We Exist

Tech shouldn’t be exclusive. PathToTech opens doors, breaks down barriers, and builds real-world opportunities that reflect the future of work—ethical, inclusive, and human-centered.
